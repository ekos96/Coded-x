Suzuka Nakamoto aka Su-Metal

Suzuka Nakamoto (Japanese: 中元 すず香, Hepburn: Nakamoto Suzuka,born in December 20, 1997) known by her stage name Su-metal, is a Japanese singer. She is best known as a member of the kawaii metal band Babymetal. She is represented by the talent agency Amuse Inc. and has been a member of three musical groups formed by the company: Karen Girl's, Sakura Gakuin, and Babymetal.
