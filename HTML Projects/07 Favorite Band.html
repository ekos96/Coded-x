Babymetal






Babymetal is a Japanese kawaii metal band consisting of Suzuka Nakamoto as "Su-metal", Moa Kikuchi as "Moametal and "Momoko Ozaki" as "Momometal". The band is produced by Kobametal from the Amuse talent agency.
Their vocals are backed by heavy metal instrumentation, performed by a group of session musicians known as the "Kami Band" at performances.
